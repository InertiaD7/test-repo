About me

Hi—I’m John Reese, a frontend developer who treats every pixel like a promise. For the past 7 years, I’ve been translating ambitious designs into lightning-fast, accessible, and delightfully interactive web experiences that work everywhere—from flagship phones to that one Windows XP machine in your office basement.

I live at the intersection of code and emotion. HTML gives structure, CSS breathes life, and JavaScript adds the spark—whether it’s a subtle hover that feels like a wink or a canvas animation that tells a story in 60 fps. I obsess over the details: the 150 ms debounce that prevents double-click chaos, the prefers-reduced-motion toggle that respects every user, the 97 Lighthouse score that loads before you blink.
